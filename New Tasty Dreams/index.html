Welcome to Tasty Dreams

Tasty Dreams is a home-style gourmet cake, pastry and savoury company where creativity and passion is the recipe for our products. Our mission is to create the awe-inspiring moment for each of our customers through our custom designed cakes, pastries and savoury treats and also our exceptional customer service. Whether you're looking for wedding cakes, kids cakes, yummy treats or just a simple quiche for dinner we will work with you to create the perfect product you need for any special occasion.

What we do

Gourmet Cakes

We make custom cakes for any occasion; wedding cakes, party cakes, kids cakes. Celebrate your special moments with one of our cakes. They look as good as they taste

Pastries

We make a wide variety of pastries for any occasion

Savoury Treats
